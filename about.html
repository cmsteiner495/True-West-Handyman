Welcome to True West Handyman

True West Handyman is a locally owned and operated business providing dependable handyman services in Colorado Springs, CO. I started this company with one clear goal: to deliver high-quality home repairs and improvements that homeowners can feel confident about. Every project is handled with care, precision, and pride—because your home deserves work done the right way.

From quick fixes to larger home maintenance and upgrade projects, I bring the same commitment to craftsmanship and customer satisfaction every time. When you hire True West Handyman, you work directly with me—the person doing the work—so communication stays simple, expectations stay clear, and there are no surprises.

My Values

Craftsmanship

I take pride in doing things the right way the first time. From careful preparation to clean finishes, I focus on quality, durability, and attention to detail. I use proven materials and methods to ensure the work holds up and looks great long after the job is done.

Customer Focus

Your home, your needs, and your peace of mind come first. I take the time to listen, answer questions, and offer honest recommendations based on what makes the most sense for your situation. My goal is simple: make sure you’re happy with both the work and the experience.

Integrity

Honest communication and fair pricing are non-negotiable. I believe in clear expectations, upfront estimates, and showing up when I say I will. You can count on straightforward advice and dependable service from start to finish.

Community Commitment

As a local business in Colorado Springs, I care deeply about the community I serve. I understand the homes, climate, and expectations of local homeowners, and I take pride in contributing to the area by providing dependable, professional service.

Why Choose True West Handyman?

Local Knowledge

Living and working in Colorado Springs gives me firsthand insight into the challenges local homes face—from weather impacts to general wear and tear. That local experience allows me to offer practical solutions that are built to last.

Versatile Services

From everyday repairs and installations to more involved improvement projects, I offer a wide range of handyman services to keep your home in great shape. If it needs fixing, adjusting, or improving, I’m here to help.

Personal, One-on-One Service

When you contact True West Handyman, you’re speaking directly with me—the person who will be doing the work. That means clear communication, consistent quality, and a level of care that only comes from a hands-on, owner-operated business.

True West Handyman is committed to helping homeowners throughout Colorado Springs, CO maintain and improve their homes with confidence. Every project, big or small, receives the same level of attention, professionalism, and respect.

I believe in making homes safer, more functional, and better looking—one project at a time. If you’re looking for dependable workmanship, honest service, and someone who takes pride in their craft, I’d be glad to earn your trust.
